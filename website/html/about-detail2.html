Message from Hon Dr. Rajitha Senarathne Minister of Health

  • June 17 2015
  • Letter to HASL

On behalf of the Ministry of Health, I hereby congratulate the Hemophilia Association of Sri Lanka (HASL) on the launch of its web site which is an important milestone for the association. Web site and other information and communication technology are valuable tool that great support the work of hemophilia organization around the world.

The HASL web site will be an important link for patients and families as it can provide them with education resources about hemophilia and related bleeding disorder, regularly inform HASL members about its events and activities, offer a platform for discussion and sharing, be a means to furnish peer support and help strengthen the bleeding disorder community nationally.

The web site will also raise awareness about hemophilia, and related bleeding disorder within Sri Lanka thereby encourages public understanding. And globally it will increase international awareness about HASL that may create opportunity for international networking with other organization. I extend my best wishes to Hemophilia Association of Sri Lanka for continued success in its efforts to improve the quality of life for patients with hemophilia and related bleeding disorders in Sri Lanka
