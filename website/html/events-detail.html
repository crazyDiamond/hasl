Care Share - A Humanitarian Aid Campaign

  • May 2015 through 2016
  • Sri Lanka

What is Care Share?

The Hemophilia Association of Sri Lanka is launching this campaign for the very first time to assist semi or fully disabled hemophilia individuals and hemophilic students in our community. This would not be possible without your donations and corporation.

Our Goal

Our goal for 2015 is to help 20 semi or fully disabled hemophiliac patients with their day to day activities and 20 students with hemophilia with their primary education.

How will your donations help them?

O Rs. 3000.00 per month (Annually Rs. 36,000.00 / US$ 270) for a fully disabled hemophilic patient

O Rs. 2000.00 per month (Annually Rs. 24,000.00 / US$ 180) for a student with hemophilia

O Non-financial benefits such as Wheel chairs, Crutches, Knee Guards, Ankle Guards

Non Financial Donations

  • Wheel Chairs
  • Crutches
  • Canes
  • Ice Packs
  • Knee Guard (L.S.M)
  • Ankle Guard (L.S.M)
  • Elbow Guard (L.S.M)
  • Commodes
  • Ice boxes (Factor Carrying)
  • Cotton Crape Bandage
